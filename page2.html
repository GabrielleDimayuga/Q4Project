Characters in Noli Me Tangere
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Ibara)
  • He is a mestiso.
  • He returned to the Philippines after studying in Europe for 7 years.
  • He is smart and intelligent.
Maria Clara
  • She is a beutiful woman in San Diego.
  • She is somehow believed to be the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and the goddaughter of Padre Damaso.
  • However, it was revealed that she was Padre Damaso's biological daughter.
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is the husband of Piya Alba.
  • He is the father of Maria Clara.
Padre Damaso
  • He is an arrogant and ruthless priest.
  • He is the Maria Clara's godfather. (Later revealed to be her biological father.)
Sisa/Narcisa
  • She is Basilio and Crispin's mother.
  • She is crazy and insane.
Basilio
  • Sisa's son and Crispin's older brother.
  • He is 10 years old.
Crispin
  • Sisa's second son and Basilio's younger brother.
  • He is 7 years old.
Elias
  • He came from a poor family.
  • He is a friend and an ally of Crisostomo Ibara.
